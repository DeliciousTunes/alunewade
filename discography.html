DISCOGRAPHY

WATERMELON MAN

Alune Wade reimagines Herbie Hancock’s 1962 classic through his Afrobeat lens, blending West African rhythms with New Orleans brass. Anchored by Wade’s signature bass and vocals, the track grooves with dynamic interplay between bass, drums, and piano, while talking drums add an authentic touch. Recorded between Paris and Lagos, this reinterpretation pays homage to Manu Dibango, bridging jazz tradition with modern Afro-jazz innovation. Produced by Wade and mixed by Nic Hard, it’s a bold tribute to the past and a fresh step into the future.

Release: 2024

Label: enja / yellowbird

Triptyque

 "Triptyque'' presents a refreshing sound that illuminates the essence of African jazz, seamlessly blending West African rhythms with modern sonic textures. Infused with Alune's characteristic groove, rooted in his mastery of the bass, the track showcases his eclectic approach to composition.

Release: 2024
