Jazzing Up the World - 
Afrobeat & Bass Groove in Cosmopolitan Fusion.

Alune Wade, hailed as a "supertalent" on bass by DLF Kultur, has graced stages alongside luminaries like Joe Zawinul and Marcus Miller. As a pivotal figure in African Jazz, the Senegalese artist skillfully amalgamates African rhythms and Jazz into eclectic, cosmopolitan compositions.
