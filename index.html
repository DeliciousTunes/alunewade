Jazzing Up the World - 
Afrobeat & Bass Groove in Cosmopolitan Fusion.

A Senegalese bass virtuoso, composer, and vocalist, Alune Wade is hailed as a "super talent" (DLF Kultur) in the global jazz and afrobeat scene. He weaves the essence of West Africa and the Middle East in his latest album 'SULTAN' . A truly cosmopolitan artist, Wade bridges Africa to Europe and the US through his music, captivating audiences with his global sound and unique musical vision.
